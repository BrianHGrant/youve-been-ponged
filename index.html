You've Been Ponged!

How to play:

  1. Enter your name and your desired number.
  2. For Pings, guess how many numbers divisble by 3 but not 5 are between 1 and your number (includes both 1 and your number).
  3. For Pongs, guess how many numbers divisble by 5 but not 3 are between 1 and your number (includes both 1 and your number).
  4. For PingPongs, guess how many numbers divisble by 3 and 5 are between 1 and your number (includes both 1 and your number).
  5. Hit Green button to play.
